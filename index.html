WELCOME to Whites Cross

All you need to know in one place.

Now a part of Cork City, but still very much in the Countryside, Whites Cross is a picturesque locality that embodies the charm and tranquility of the Irish countryside. Located just a short drive from Cork City centre, it offers visitors a delightful mix of rural serenity and easy access to the bustling urban life of one of Ireland's most dynamic cities.
